Exercise 4

You work in an Italian restaurant and you will be making the classic marinara sauce. Put the following steps for making classic marinara sauce in order.

Drag and drop each item until the steps are in the correct order.

  • Maintain a temperature of 135°F until ready to serve. (6)
  • Prep the ingredients for use. (4)
  • Serve the sauce and let your customers enjoy. (9)
  • Receive the delivery of the ingredients. (2)
  • Put remaining sauce in shallow pan to cool and put in the refrigerator. (7)
  • Reheat to 165°F for 15 seconds to serve the sauce again. (8)
  • Order the needed ingredients from an approved provider. (1)
  • Combine all ingredients and cook until a temperature of 145°F has been achieved. (5)
  • Properly check-in and store the ingredients until ready to use. (3)
